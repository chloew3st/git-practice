Hi, my name is Chloé and I have some advice for you!

Learning how to troubleshoot mistakes is the whole job!

Coding is more than design and analytics, it's a journey of learning how to harness imperfection. Trouble-shooting is like 80% of the job (maybe even more, if I'm honest). Like anything worth doing, coding is challenging and rewarding once the hours of hard work finally pay off!

Teamwork is essential!

As tempting as it is to promote yourself to "god" status after learning all these incredible skills, it's important to remember that you're not in a vacuum. Life is full of "team spirit" in most activities and coding is no exception. Release that ego, embrace your inner humble-pie and get ready to play in the big leagues!

Don't beat yourself up!

Make sure to be patient with yourself in this process. Give yourself grace and be willing to step away for a minute to reassess any problems that aren't resolving themselves after repeated attempts. Sometimes all you need is a fresh perspective. So go take a walk and try again later!
